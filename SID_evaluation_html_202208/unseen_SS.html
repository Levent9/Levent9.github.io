Evaluation of Synthetic Speech Based on Silent Face Video

We are gathering people's opinions on the output of several different speech synthesis systems that generate speech from silent face video. You will be performing a series of evaluations of the speech produced by these systems. Please watch the video files below by clicking on the icons. After watch the video, you will choose a score for the video file you've just watched and heard. This score should reflect your opinion whether the face and sound in the video are consistent. Specifically, focus on the timebre of the sound rather than the content. The scale is from 1 [completely inconsistent] to 5 [completely consistent].

Instructions:

  • Please watch and listen the video with headphones.
  • In order to complete the task, you had better use Chrome, IE10, or IE11 browser.
  • Please select a proper score from 1-5 for each sentence, with 1 representing completely unnatural and 5 standing for completely natural. Don't miss any one, or we will refuse your answer.
  • You should not judge the grammar or content of the sentence but just how face and voice are consistent.
    • In an extreme case, if there is a male face in the video, but a female voice, this is obviously inconsistent, so the score should low.
  • Are you a native English speaker from Britain, America, or other English speaking contries (please type your contry)

    If you are not a native English speaker, we will refuse your anwser.

  • It is obvious that the face in first one is more consistent with sound in the video, and the second is worse. So you should give the first audio a higher score than the second one.
