Evaluation of Synthetic Speech Based on Silent Face Video

We are gathering people's opinions on the output of several different speech synthesis systems that generate speech from silent face video. You will be performing a series of evaluations of the speech produced by these systems. Please listen to the audio files below by clicking on the icons. After listening to each sentence, you will choose a score for the audio file you've just heard. This score should reflect your opinion of how natural or unnatural the sentence sounded. The scale is from 1 [completely unnatural] to 5 [completely natural].

Instructions:

  • Please listen with headphones.
  • In order to complete the task, you had better use Chrome, IE10, or IE11 browser.
  • Please select a proper score from 1-5 for each sentence, with 1 representing completely unnatural and 5 standing for completely natural. Don't miss any one, or we will refuse your answer.
  • You should not judge the grammar, content or timbre of the sentence but just how natural and intelligible it sounds.
  • Are you a native English speaker from Britain, America, or other English speaking contries (please type your contry)

    If you are not a native English speaker, we will refuse your anwser.
