Evaluation of Converted Speech Based on the face image

We are gathering people's opinions on the output of several voice conversion systems that the face of target speaker is used to control the voice characteristic.. You will be performing a series of evaluations of the speech produced by these systems. Please listen to the audio files below by clicking on the icons. After listening to each sentence, you will choose a score for the audio file you've just heard. This score should reflect your opinion whether the face and speech are congruous. Specifically, focus on the timebre of the sound rather than the content. The scale is from 1 [completely inconsistent] to 5 [completely consistent].

Instructions:

  • Please watch the face image and listen the speech.
  • In order to complete the task, you had better use Chrome, IE10, or IE11 browser.
  • Please select a proper score from 1-5 for each sentence, with 1 representing completely incongruous and 5 standing for completely congruous. Don't miss any one, or we will refuse your answer.
  • You should not judge the grammar or content of the sentence but just how face and voice are congruous.
    • In an extreme case, if there is a male face in the video, but a female voice, this is obviously inconsistent, so the score should low.
  • Are you a native English speaker from Britain, America, or other English speaking contries (please type your contry)

    If you are not a native English speaker, we will refuse your anwser.
